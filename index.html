About Me

My name is Owen Wilkins and I am a 1st Semester, 2nd Term Graphic Design student at Algonquin College. So far I have been enjoy many of my classes and have been noticing my skills improving. I decided to go to school for Graphic Design as I had been a designer through most of my elementary and high-school years, creating basic things like custom presentation slide layouts as well as having a love of art. These things pushed me towards the Program where I hoped to improve these skills to a professional level. I have experienced a fair amount of coding during high-school, mainly in the languages Python and JavaScript. I am excited to learn more about design focused coding in this course.

Outside of school I enjoy playing games and talking with friends, reading sci-fi books, and interacting with online communities like twich. My favourite seaon is winter as I am a fan of the longer nights when I am most productive, and the colder days. As I often tell people You can always put on more layers when it gets colder, but there are only so many You can take off when it gets too warm. 03, 11, and 13 are my favourite numbers as I have fun connections to each of them. If I had to choose a favourite food it would probably be Gyro's or Spicy Curry.

My motivational quote is: ...I can't get there yet, but it looks like I have learned - Eric (PointCrow) Morino
